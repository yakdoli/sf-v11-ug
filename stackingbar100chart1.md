#### Stacking Bar 100 Chart {#stacking-bar-100-chart style="tab-stops: 0pt"}

[]{#p28}[]{style="COLOR: #15428b"} 

The Stacking Bar 100 Chart is similar to the Stacking Bar Chart. The only difference is that the total Y value is converted to a 100 percent proportional value.

[]{style="COLOR: #15428b"}
