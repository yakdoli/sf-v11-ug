#### Client-Side Methods {#client-side-methods style="tab-stops: 0pt"}

The following section details the client-side methods supported by the Ribbon control.

  ShowContextTab(name)                                          [Shows the contextual tab for the selected items in the page.]{style="COLOR: black"}   [Name of the contextual tab.]{style="COLOR: black"}               [NA]{style="COLOR: black"}
  ShowTab(name)                                                 [Shows the ribbon tab.]{style="COLOR: black"}                                          [Name of the ribbon tab.]{style="COLOR: black"}                   [NA]{style="COLOR: black"}
