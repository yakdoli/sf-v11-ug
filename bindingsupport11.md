#### Binding Support {#binding-support style="tab-stops: 0pt"}

 

Data binding is the process of establishing a connection between the application UI and business logic. Data binding can be unidirectional (source to target or target to source) or bidirectional (source to target and target to source). You can bind the data to the MaskedTextBox through the Value property.

[      ]{style="FONT-FAMILY: 'Trebuchet MS','sans-serif'; COLOR: #15428b; FONT-SIZE: 9pt"}
