#### Zoom with[[ ]{style="FONT-WEIGHT: normal"}]{.Heading4Char}Zoom Mode {#zoom-with-zoom-mode style="tab-stops: 0pt"}

There are two ZoomModes available in the Map Control:

[·      ]{style="FONT-FAMILY: Symbol"}SingleClick

[·      ]{style="FONT-FAMILY: Symbol"}DoubleClick

 

Select Zoom mode as SingleClick and click on the Map, ZoomedIn.

Select Zoom mode as DoubleClick and click twice on the Map ZoomedIn.
