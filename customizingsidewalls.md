##### Customizing Side Walls {#customizing-side-walls style="tab-stops: 0pt"}

3D chart allows the user to customize the look and feel of the side walls in the 3D chart. Exclusive properties has been implemented to customize the look and feel of the side walls. The following are properties that can be used to add look and feel to 3D chart.

 

[·      ]{style="FONT-FAMILY: Symbol"}**ShowLeftWall**-This property is used to display or hide the left side wall

[·      ]{style="FONT-FAMILY: Symbol"}**ShowBackWall**-This property is used to display or hide the back wall of the 3D chart

[·      ]{style="FONT-FAMILY: Symbol"}**ShowBottomWall**-This property is used to display or hide the bottom wall of the 3D chart

[·      ]{style="FONT-FAMILY: Symbol"}**BackWallBackground**-This property is used to customize the look and feel of the back wall

[·      ]{style="FONT-FAMILY: Symbol"}**LeftWallBackground**-This property is used to customize the look and feel of the left wall

[·      ]{style="FONT-FAMILY: Symbol"}**BottomWallBackground**-This property is used to customize the look and feel of the bottom wall

[]{style="FONT-FAMILY: 'Trebuchet MS','sans-serif'; COLOR: #15428b; FONT-SIZE: 9pt"}
