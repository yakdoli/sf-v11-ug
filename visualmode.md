#### Visual Mode {#visual-mode style="tab-stops: 0pt"}

 

Gallery control supports two types of visual modes. They are as follows.

 

[·      ]{style="FONT-FAMILY: Symbol"}Standard

[·      ]{style="FONT-FAMILY: Symbol"}Detailed
