### Handling the Reminder Window {#handling-the-reminder-window style="LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; tab-stops: 0pt"}

You have three options for handling the reminder window.

[·      ]{style="FONT-FAMILY: Symbol"}**Dismiss** -- Dismisses the selected appointment and will not remind you again.

[·      ]{style="FONT-FAMILY: Symbol"}**Dismiss All** -- Dismisses all the appointments and will not remind you again.

[·      ]{style="FONT-FAMILY: Symbol"}**Snooze** -- Selected appointments will be reminded again after a selected interval of time.
