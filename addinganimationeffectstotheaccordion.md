#### Adding Animation Effects to the Accordion {#adding-animation-effects-to-the-accordion style="tab-stops: 0pt"}

Accordion supports built-in animation effects for the expand and collapse actions.[ ]{style="FONT-FAMILY: 'Times New Roman','serif'; FONT-SIZE: 12pt"}
