##### Adding Labels to the Linear Gauge {#adding-labels-to-the-linear-gauge style="tab-stops: 0pt"}

[]{style="FONT-FAMILY: 'Trebuchet MS','sans-serif'; COLOR: #15428b; FONT-SIZE: 9pt"} 

Essential Gauge Silverlight provides support to display custom text in the Linear Gauge control. This feature enables to display any specific information related to the value being measured, or company name, or units, and so on.

[]{style="FONT-FAMILY: 'Trebuchet MS','sans-serif'; COLOR: #15428b; FONT-SIZE: 9pt"} 

You can also change the orientation and alignment of the text.

[]{style="FONT-FAMILY: 'Trebuchet MS','sans-serif'; COLOR: #15428b; FONT-SIZE: 9pt"}
