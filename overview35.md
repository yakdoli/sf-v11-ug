### Overview {#overview style="tab-stops: 0pt"}

Zooming and Panning feature in Chart enables you to view the chart content across a range of magnification. It is used to render the chart based on the zooming level, for better visualization of data. Generally, the zooming level will be calculated based on the Zoom Factor property.
