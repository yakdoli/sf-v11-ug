##### Rotator with Button Mode {#rotator-with-button-mode style="tab-stops: 0pt"}

###### 5.6.2.2.6.1 Introduction {#introduction style="tab-stops: 0pt"}

The Rotator control with button mode allows users to browse through a set of items by clicking the buttons. The items can be moved in up or down and left or right directions by clicking the corresponding buttons.[]{style="FONT-SIZE: 9pt"}

###### 5.6.2.2.6.2 Use Case Scenarios {#use-case-scenarios style="tab-stops: 0pt"}
