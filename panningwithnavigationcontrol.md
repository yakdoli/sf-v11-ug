### Panning with Navigation Control {#panning-with-navigation-control style="tab-stops: 0pt"}

 

Maps can be panned with Pan buttons such as TopPan button, RightPan button, BottomPan button and LeftPan button. When clicking a particular Pan button, the Map will be panned in the respective directions.
