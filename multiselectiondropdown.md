### MultiSelectionDropDown {#multiselectiondropdown style="tab-stops: 0pt"}

 

MultiSelectionDropDown control is a dropdown list box / combo box that allows to display the dropdown with check boxes in items. Multiple items can be selected by toggling the check boxes. The selected items will be displayed in the combo textbox separated by the delimiter.
