## Use Case Scenarios {#use-case-scenarios style="tab-stops: 0pt"}

Spreadsheet control enables you to create, view and manipulate large Excel documents in your application quickly and efficiently. It is not mandatory to depend on offline application.

You can use this in sales tracking application to generate and customize a report on sales figures.

You can also use this in a banking website to allow the users to customize monthly statements and save it in Microsoft Excel format.
