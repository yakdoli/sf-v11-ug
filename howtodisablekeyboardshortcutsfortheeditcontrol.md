## How To Disable Keyboard Shortcuts For the Edit Control {#how-to-disable-keyboard-shortcuts-for-the-edit-control style="tab-stops: 0pt"}

[]{style="FONT-FAMILY: 'Trebuchet MS','sans-serif'; COLOR: #15428b; FONT-SIZE: 9pt"}
