##### Triggering Actions while closing the TDI / MDI items {#triggering-actions-while-closing-the-tdi-mdi-items style="tab-stops: 0pt"}

 

In the DockingManager with MDI / TDI children, there will be every necessity to trigger some actions when you close the tabs. The following events are certainly very handy when you close the tabs.

 

[·      ]{style="FONT-FAMILY: Symbol"}CloseButtonClick -- This event is handled when you click the close button on the element in the **Document** state.
