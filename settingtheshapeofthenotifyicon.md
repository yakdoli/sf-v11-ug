#### Setting the Shape of the Notify Icon {#setting-the-shape-of-the-notify-icon style="tab-stops: 0pt"}

[]{style="FONT-FAMILY: 'Trebuchet MS','sans-serif'; COLOR: #15428b; FONT-SIZE: 9pt"} 

NotifyIcon can take the following three different shapes.

[]{style="FONT-FAMILY: 'Trebuchet MS','sans-serif'; COLOR: #15428b; FONT-SIZE: 9pt"} 

[·      ]{style="FONT-FAMILY: Symbol"}Balloon

[·      ]{style="FONT-FAMILY: Symbol"}Rectangle

[·      ]{style="FONT-FAMILY: Symbol"}RoundedRectangle

[]{style="FONT-FAMILY: 'Trebuchet MS','sans-serif'; COLOR: #15428b; FONT-SIZE: 9pt"}
