Zooming and Scrolling

Essential Grid supports interactive features like zooming and scrolling. It supports zooming at the cell and grid level with and without in-built support. You can also use detached scrollbars with Essential Grid. Thus, you can drop scrollbars on your form and tell a grid to use them instead of the grid’s standard scrollbars.
