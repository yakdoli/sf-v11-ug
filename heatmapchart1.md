### HeatMap Chart {#heatmap-chart style="tab-stops: 0pt"}

A HeatMap chart is a graphical representation of data where the values taken by a variable in a two-dimensional map are represented as colors.  

HeatMap chart can be created through two ways:

[·      ]{style="FONT-FAMILY: Symbol"}Builder

[·      ]{style="FONT-FAMILY: Symbol"}ChartModel

 

Chart Details

 

::: {align="center"}
+------------------------------+-----------------------+
| Details                                              |
+------------------------------+-----------------------+
| Number of Y values per point | 2                     |
+------------------------------+-----------------------+
| Number of Series             | One                   |
+------------------------------+-----------------------+
| Cannot be Combined with      | Any other chart type. |
+------------------------------+-----------------------+
:::
