#### How to expand or collapse all the child nodes of a particular node {#how-to-expand-or-collapse-all-the-child-nodes-of-a-particular-node style="MARGIN-LEFT: 18pt; tab-stops: 18.0pt"}

[]{style="COLOR: #15428b"} 

All the treeview nodes can be collapsed by calling the **TreeViewAdv.CollapseAll** method. For individual nodes, call **Node.Collapse** method.
