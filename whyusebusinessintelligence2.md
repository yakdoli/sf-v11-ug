### Why use Business Intelligence? {#why-use-business-intelligence style="tab-stops: 0pt"}

[]{style="FONT-FAMILY: 'Calibri','sans-serif'; COLOR: black; FONT-SIZE: 11pt"} 

It\'s hard to emphasize the importance of Business Intelligence (BI) in today\'s world. Strategic business decisions are impossible to make without analyzing the past business performance. Businesses are increasingly investing heavily in tools and services that allow decision makers to visually analyze data in many ways.

Several products and solutions have emerged to cater to the BI market. Lessons have been learnt and currently OLAP is the de facto standard for persisting and accessing BI data. Applications built by using the OLAP include sales reports, executive reports, forecasting, etc.
