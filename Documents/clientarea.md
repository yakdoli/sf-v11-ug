#### []{#_Ref265161519}[]{#_Client_Area}Client Area

[]{style="FONT-FAMILY: 'Calibri','sans-serif'"} 

DockingManager provides option for the users to control the hosting of a Client control. A control can be displayed in client area by setting **SideInDockedMode** to None. The main purpose of client control is resizing and repositioning based on other docking windows[.]{style="FONT-SIZE: 14pt"}
