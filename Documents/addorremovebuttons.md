#### Add or Remove buttons

ToolBarAdv provides option to show or hide an item. By default, Add or Remove Button will be listed in the overflow popup. When you click Add or Remove Button, a popup will display the items available in ToolBarAdv. Unselect the respective checkboxes of the items you want to hide.

 

**Adding an Item to Add or Remove Button**

You can add an item to Add or Remove Buttons, setting the ToolBarAdv.Icon and ToolBarAdv.Label properties.
