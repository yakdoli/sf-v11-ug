#### Culture and Format properties {#culture-and-format-properties style="tab-stops: 0pt"}

MaskedTextBox provides globalization support through the Culture property. The CurrencySymbol, DateSeparator, DecimalSeparator, TimeSeparator, and NumberGroupSeparator are based on the Culture property.

As you have seen in these samples whenever you change the Culture property the Value is formatted based on the Culture.

MaskedTextBox supports direct properties such as the CurrencySymbol, DateSeparator, DecimalSeparator, TimeSeparator, and NumberGroupSeparator.
