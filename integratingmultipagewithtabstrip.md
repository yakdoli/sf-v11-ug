##### Integrating Multipage with Tabstrip {#integrating-multipage-with-tabstrip style="tab-stops: 0pt"}

[]{style="FONT-FAMILY: 'Trebuchet MS','sans-serif'; COLOR: #15428b; FONT-SIZE: 9pt"}
