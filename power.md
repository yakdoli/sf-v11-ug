### POWER {#power style="tab-stops: 0pt"}

 

Returns the result of a number raised to a power.

 

**Syntax**

 

**POWER(number, power)**

 

where:

**number** is the base number. It can be any real number.

**power** is the exponent to which, the base number is raised.
