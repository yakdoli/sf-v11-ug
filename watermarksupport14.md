#### WaterMark Support {#watermark-support style="TEXT-JUSTIFY: inter-ideograph; TEXT-ALIGN: justify; tab-stops: 0pt"}

A watermark is the dummy content displayed in the MaskEditTextBox when the value string is empty. The WaterMarkText support behavior can be enabled by setting the **WaterMarkTextIsVisible** property to true.
