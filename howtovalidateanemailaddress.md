#### How to validate an email address {#how-to-validate-an-email-address style="tab-stops: 0pt"}

 

You can validate the email address by using the ValidatingString property. You have to set the regular expression of the email address to the ValidationString property and then the text entered by the end-user is validated based on the Validation string given by you.
