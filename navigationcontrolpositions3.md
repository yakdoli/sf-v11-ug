### Navigation Control Positions {#navigation-control-positions style="tab-stops: 0pt"}

Navigation control can be positioned at following places on the Maps control.

[·      ]{style="FONT-FAMILY: Symbol"}Top

[·      ]{style="FONT-FAMILY: Symbol"}Right

[·      ]{style="FONT-FAMILY: Symbol"}Bottom

[·      ]{style="FONT-FAMILY: Symbol"}Left
