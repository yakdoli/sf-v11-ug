#### Tick Placement {#tick-placement style="tab-stops: 0pt"}

[]{style="FONT-FAMILY: 'Trebuchet MS','sans-serif'; COLOR: #15428b; FONT-SIZE: 9pt"} 

The **TickPlacement** property specifies the placement of Ticks and Labels around the control. The position can be specified:

[]{style="FONT-FAMILY: 'Trebuchet MS','sans-serif'; COLOR: #15428b; FONT-SIZE: 9pt"} 

[·      ]{style="FONT-FAMILY: Symbol"}Top or Down for Horizontal orientation

[·      ]{style="FONT-FAMILY: Symbol"}Left or Right for vertical orientation

[·      ]{style="FONT-FAMILY: Symbol"}Both or None for both the orientations

[]{style="FONT-FAMILY: 'Trebuchet MS','sans-serif'; COLOR: #15428b; FONT-SIZE: 9pt"}
