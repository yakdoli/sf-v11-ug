## Shapes Customization {#shapes-customization style="tab-stops: 0pt"}

 

Shapes in the Map Control can be customized by ShapeFill, ShapeStroke and  ShapeStrokeThickness

 

ShapeFill, ShapeStroke, and ShapeStrokeThickness

The *ShapeFill* property will fill the shape with the respective color. The *ShapeStroke* property will set the border color of the shape in the Map. And the *ShapeStrokeThickness* property will set the thickness of the shape's border.
