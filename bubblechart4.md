#### Bubble Chart {#bubble-chart style="tab-stops: 0pt"}

[]{style="FONT-FAMILY: 'Trebuchet MS','sans-serif'; COLOR: #15428b; FONT-SIZE: 9pt"} 

Bubble Chart is an extension of the Scatter Chart (or XY Chart) where each data marker is represented by a circle whose dimension forms a third variable. Consequently, bubble charts allow three-variable comparisons, allowing easy visualization of complex interdependencies that are not apparent in two-variable charts. Bubble charts are frequently used in market and product comparison studies.
