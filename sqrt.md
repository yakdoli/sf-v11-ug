### SQRT {#sqrt style="tab-stops: 0pt"}

 

Returns a positive square root.

 

**Syntax**

 

**SQRT(number)**

 

where:

**number** is the number for which you want the square root.

 

**Remarks**

 

[·      ]{style="FONT-FAMILY: Symbol"}Number must be \>= 0.
