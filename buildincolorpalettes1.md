### Build in ColorPalettes {#build-in-colorpalettes style="tab-stops: 0pt"}

 

ColorPalette is similar to ShapeFill, ShapeStroke and ShapeStrokeThickness. The different between Color Palettes and ShapeFill, ShapeStroke and ShapeStrokeThickness is, the ColorPalette is set of predefined colors and you can apply different colors for each shape in the Map. But if ShapeFill, ShapeStroke and ShapeStrokeThickness are used, you can only apply same color to all the shapes in the Map.

 

 

The following color palettes are available in Maps control:

 

[·      ]{style="FONT-FAMILY: Symbol"}None

[·      ]{style="FONT-FAMILY: Symbol"}ColorPalette1

[·      ]{style="FONT-FAMILY: Symbol"}ColorPalette2

[·      ]{style="FONT-FAMILY: Symbol"}ColorPalette3

[·      ]{style="FONT-FAMILY: Symbol"}ColorPalette4

[·      ]{style="FONT-FAMILY: Symbol"}ColorPalette5

[·      ]{style="FONT-FAMILY: Symbol"}CustomColorPalettePalette
