### P - Paragraph Tag {#p---paragraph-tag style="tab-stops: 0pt"}

[]{style="FONT-FAMILY: 'Trebuchet MS','sans-serif'; COLOR: #15428b; FONT-SIZE: 9pt"} 

The **P** tag is used to define a paragraph in the HTML document. HTMLUI supports the P tag along with its align attribute. The **align** attribute is used to specify the alignment of the text within the paragraph. The alignment values are as follows:

[]{style="FONT-FAMILY: 'Trebuchet MS','sans-serif'; COLOR: #15428b; FONT-SIZE: 9pt"} 

[·      ]{style="FONT-FAMILY: Symbol"}**left**: Left-aligns the text

[·      ]{style="FONT-FAMILY: Symbol"}**right**: Right-aligns the text

[·      ]{style="FONT-FAMILY: Symbol"}**center**: Positions text at the center of the document

[·      ]{style="FONT-FAMILY: Symbol"}**justify**: Left-aligns and right-aligns text
