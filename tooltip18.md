## ToolTip {#tooltip style="tab-stops: 0pt"}

Use Case Scenario[[]{style="FONT-SIZE: 16pt; FONT-WEIGHT: normal"}]{.Heading3Char}

ToolTip helps users to view the Measure and Dimension information of cells quickly.
