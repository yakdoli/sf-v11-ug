### Accumulation Charts {#accumulation-charts style="tab-stops: 0pt"}

Accumulation charts are typically single series charts representing the data as portions of hundred percent and do not use any axes. Essential Chart offers the following two types of Accumulation charts:

[·      ]{style="FONT-FAMILY: Symbol"}Funnel chart
