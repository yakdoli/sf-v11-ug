### License Agreement {#license-agreement style="tab-stops: 0pt"}

The license agreement gives you information on terms and conditions you will need to be aware of, and agree to when you are ready to use Essential Studio Product. You can access the license agreement from Dashboard. 

The following are the steps to open the License Agreement:

[]{style="FONT-FAMILY: 'Trebuchet MS','sans-serif'; COLOR: #15428b; FONT-SIZE: 9pt"} 

1.   Open Syncfusion **Dashboard.**

2.   Click **License Management**.

3.   Click **Open** button for **License Agreement**. The **Software License Agreement** will open.
