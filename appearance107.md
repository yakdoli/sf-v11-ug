##### Appearance {#appearance style="tab-stops: 0pt"}

The RadioButton and RadioButtonFor controls support built-in themes that provide high visual appeal and are suitable for various layouts. They support the following four built-in Syncfusion themes to enhance the look and feel:

[·      ]{style="FONT-FAMILY: Symbol"} [BlueLight]{style="FONT-FAMILY: 'Arial','sans-serif'"}

[·      ]{style="FONT-FAMILY: Symbol"} [DarkNight]{style="FONT-FAMILY: 'Arial','sans-serif'"}

[·      ]{style="FONT-FAMILY: Symbol"} [MetroBlue]{style="FONT-FAMILY: 'Arial','sans-serif'"}

[·      ]{style="FONT-FAMILY: Symbol"} [Spinach]{style="FONT-FAMILY: 'Arial','sans-serif'"}

Using Builder

The following steps explain how to customize the appearance of the RadioButton and RadioButtonFor controls using Builder.

1.   In the **view**, invoke the **RadioButton** and **RadioButtonFor** helpers with the group name of the radio button as the first argument, followed by the **AutoFormat** method with the desired theme as its argument.
